The story behind Operation Maths

Operation Maths grew out of my work as a private tutor. Session after session I found myself returning to the same topics - the areas where pupils consistently lose marks in the SATs - and developing resources to address them clearly and effectively.

Over time those resources built up into a substantial bank of worksheets, support sheets and teaching materials. It made sense to make them available more widely, so that pupils and parents could access the same quality of material whether or not they were having one-to-one tuition.

Everything on the site is designed with a real classroom and real children in mind. There are no shortcuts or gimmicks - just clear teaching, well-structured practice and the confidence that comes from understanding rather than just memorising.
